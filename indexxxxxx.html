4. Squid game

You can challenge the children to try this brain-teasing and fun game. The rule of this ADHD game is simple: when someone goes when they should stop, they are out of the game. The first one to hit the finish line wins.

5. Enemy Squash

Enemy Squash is a beginner friendly shooting game. The game interface is kept simple and straightforward so that even colorblind players can enjoy the game without having to focus too much on the colours.

Step 1:Shooter will receive 10 points for killing each adversary, Shooter can use as much [Light Ammo] as he/she desire.

Step 2:Each [Heavy Ammo] shot imposes penalty of 2 points. To use the [Heavy Ammo], shooter must have a minimum of two points. [Mega Strike] costs 25 points . To use the [Mega Strike], shooter must have at least 25 points.

Step 3:[Light Ammo] is swift yet less powerful. [Heavy Ammo] are slower but do more damage.

Step 4:Each opponent is promptly killed by a [Mega Strike].

Game Controls:

            1. Attack with the [Light Ammo] by using the left mouse button.

           2. To assault with a [Heavy Ammo], right-click.

           3. To assault with a [Mega Strike], press the Spacebar.

10. Blits Raven

Welcome to the 'Blitz Raven' mouse-clicking game! The player's aim in this game is to safeguard the village from ravens that are attacking. To play the game, simply click on the screen to shoot at the ravens and keep them from reaching your hamlet.
